# Domain & MoIT Compliance Guide: Personal to Corporate

To comply with Vietnam's Ministry of Industry and Trade (MoIT) standards for the website `tungbachbds.vn`, you must transition the ownership and registration status.

## 1. Domain Transfer Checklist (Cá nhân -> Doanh nghiệp)
This process is technically a "Chuyển nhượng quyền sử dụng tên miền" (Transfer of usage rights).

### Information to Request from Team/Legal:
- [ ] **Bản khai đăng ký tên miền:** A registration form for the company (the registrar will provide this).
- [ ] **Giấy phép kinh doanh (GPKD):** A scanned copy of the company's Business Registration License.
- [ ] **CCCD của người đại diện:** A copy of the ID card of the legal representative of Công ty TNHH Tùng Bách.
- [ ] **Văn bản đề nghị chuyển nhượng:** A transfer request document signed by you (current owner) and signed/stamped by the company director.

### Estimated Fees:
- **Transfer Fee:** ~500,000 - 1,000,000 VND (depends on the registrar like iNET, Mắt Bão, or P.A Việt Nam).
- **Maintenance Fee:** You may need to pay for an additional year of renewal during the transfer.

## 2. MoIT Notification Requirements (online.gov.vn)
Once the domain is in the company's name, we must notify the MoIT. The site MUST display:

### Information to Request from Team:
- [ ] **Mã số thuế (Tax ID):** The official tax identification number.
- [ ] **Địa chỉ trụ sở:** The official registered business address.
- [ ] **Số điện thoại/Email:** Official contact details for the company.
- [ ] **Hồ sơ pháp lý dự án:** We need the project approval documents for "Nhà ở xã hội Tùng Bách" to link in the legal section (ensures transparency).
